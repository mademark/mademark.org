Make Your AI Transparent

Set up your AI to automatically label generated content with Made Mark — takes 2 minutes, works forever.

Custom instructions are a standing note your AI reads before every response. Paste one of the blocks below into your platform's settings once, and it will keep appending a Made Mark attribution to publishable work — articles, posts, emails, reports — without you asking again. This page exists because authorship transparency should be the default, not something you remember to do by hand each time.

Claude (Anthropic)

Where this goes: claude.ai → Settings → Profile → Custom instructions.

When generating written content (articles, blog posts, social media
posts, emails, reports, or any publishable text), automatically append
this attribution at the end of your response:

🤖 AI Made
This content was primarily conceived and produced by Claude (Anthropic)
Made with AI: https://mademark.org/ai-made

If the user provided significant creative direction, shaping the
content's intent while I produced it, use this instead:

👤🤖 Human Designed, AI Made
This content was creatively directed by [Author Name] and produced by
Claude (Anthropic)
Made with AI: https://mademark.org/human-designed-ai-made

Use your judgment based on the level of creative direction. When in
doubt, use "Human Designed, AI Made."

Do not add attribution to: conversational responses, code snippets,
data analysis, or answers to questions. Only add attribution to content
intended for publication or sharing.

Frequently asked questions

Will this add attribution to everything I ask my AI?

No. Only to content intended for publication (articles, posts, reports). Not to conversational responses, code, or data analysis.

What's the difference between "AI Made" and "Human Designed, AI Made"?

"AI Made" means AI conceived and produced the content with no creative direction. "Human Designed, AI Made" means you shaped the creative intent and direction while AI executed the production. If you're guiding what to say, use the latter.

Can I customize the attribution text?

Yes. You can edit [Author Name] to your actual name, or modify any part of the instruction to fit your needs.

Can I turn this off?

Yes. Simply delete the custom instruction from your AI's settings and it will stop appending attribution.

Does this work with API usage?

Only if you're using the web interface. API users would need to add attribution in their application code.

What if attribution appears on responses where I don't want it?

The instructions are designed to only apply to publishable content. If it's appearing too often, you can refine the instruction by making the qualifier more specific.
