How to use this label

      

        
On a webpage — paste the JSON-LD tab output into your HTML head. Invisible to readers, machine-readable by everything else.

      

      

        
On an image or video — add the XMP tab output to your file using ExifTool or any XMP-aware application.

      

      

        
In a C2PA manifest — include the C2PA tab output in your signing workflow. Share with your engineering team for platform integrations.

      

    


    

      
What happens to your data

      
Nothing. This generator runs entirely in your browser. No information is sent to any server. Made Mark does not collect, store, or transmit any data you enter here.
