Label images

Embed a Made Mark label into a JPEG or PNG. The label is written into the file's XMP metadata, with an optional visible badge drawn onto the image. Everything runs in your browser — your image never leaves your device.

1 · Your image (JPEG or PNG)

Drop an image here or click to choose a file

2 · How was this image made? (required)

3 · Visible badge (optional)

Draws the mark icon onto a corner of the image. Without a badge the label is metadata-only and your image bytes are untouched apart from the XMP block — no re-encoding, no quality loss.

JPEGs are re-encoded at high quality when a badge is drawn; PNGs stay lossless either way.

4 · Download

Verify the embedded label with exiftool -XMP-mm:all yourfile.jpg, or inspect it in any XMP-aware tool. Some platforms strip metadata on upload — that's a platform behavior, not a Made Mark limitation.
