Human Made

Canonical URI https://mademark.org/marks/human-made

Conceived and produced by people. No AI tools involved in the creative work itself.

A photograph. A hand-lettered poster. A novel written without AI assistance.

Use this mark when

The creator made every significant creative decision.

AI tools were not used in producing the work.

The work could have been made the same way before AI existed.
